03. How long does it take to create a website?
How long will it take for you to complete the order for the creation of the site?

The lead time depends on the complexity of the project. A simple site-visit, we do about one week. The implementation of sites of medium complexity - one or two weeks. Implementation of complex projects can take up to one to two months.
